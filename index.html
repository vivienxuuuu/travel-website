Why I Started This

I've always been someone who gets excited about new places and different cultures. After studying abroad and taking some pretty amazing trips, I realized I wanted a place to collect all these memories and stories.

This website is basically my digital scrapbook - somewhere I can look back on all the places I've been and get excited about where I'm going next. If my stories inspire you to travel somewhere new, that's just a bonus!

My Time in America

Living in New York for my exchange at NYU was incredible - the energy of Manhattan never gets old! The highlight was definitely that crazy 40-hour train ride from Chicago to San Francisco. Watching America change through the window was like flipping through a giant photo book. New Orleans had the best jazz and food, Seattle's coffee scene was amazing, and Yellowstone just blew my mind with its natural beauty.

Exploring Europe

Edinburgh was my home base for grad school - I loved how the old castle sits right next to modern university buildings. From there, I explored so many amazing places. Prague felt like a fairy tale, Copenhagen was all about cozy cafes and bikes, and Italy... wow. Rome's history is everywhere you look, and Naples has the most authentic pizza I've ever had. Each country had its own vibe and stories to tell.

Discovering Japan

Japan completely changed how I think about travel and culture. Everything there is so thoughtfully designed - from the train stations to the tiny ramen shops. The way they blend super modern technology with ancient traditions is fascinating. I spent hours just walking around Tokyo, trying different foods, and getting lost in temples. It made me realize how much more of Asia I want to explore.
